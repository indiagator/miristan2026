Our Story

Miristan was formed to operate inside environments where opportunity exists, but execution is difficult. As trade, food systems, and industrial activity across Eurasia expanded, success depended on coordinating people, processes, and ground realities across borders.

From the beginning, Miristan functioned as a consulting and services group, working directly with businesses and operators across India, Russia, and the wider Eurasian region.

Over time, food systems, trade routes, manpower deployment, and operational security emerged as interconnected requirements of execution.

Founder

Miristan was founded by an entrepreneur and operator with sustained, hands-on experience across India–Russia and the wider Eurasian region. The founding work emerged from direct engagement with exporters, small businesses, farmers, service providers, and institutions.

The founder’s approach has consistently been execution-led—covering market studies, trade facilitation, farming initiatives, deal negotiation, logistics coordination, manpower alignment, and on-ground operational setup.

This depth of exposure across food systems, trade, services, and cross-border operations continues to shape Miristan as a consulting and services group built for continuity in complex, high-friction environments.
